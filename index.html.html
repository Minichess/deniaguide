The bestbeaches.

Les Rotes

Dénia

Wild rocky coves south of Dénia. Crystal-clear water and great snorkelling. No facilities. Arrive early.

Google Maps

Cala Granadella

Jávea

One of the most spectacular coves on the Costa Blanca. Intense turquoise water. Arrive before 9:30h in July.

Google Maps

Playa de la Barraca

Jávea

Quiet and uncrowded. Pebble seabed with exceptional visibility — one of Jávea's best snorkel spots.

Jávea

Small, well-preserved cove near Lulu Beachclub. Walkable from town. Go early in summer.

Section four

Markets &villages.

Mercat Municipal de Dénia

Mon–Sat · 8h–14h

The most authentic market in the area. Fresh fish, local fruit, butchers, flowers and bars inside for breakfast among the stalls.

Google Maps

Mercadillo de Pedreguer

Sundays · 8h–13h · 10 min from Dénia

The best inland market in the Marina Alta. Antiques, local crafts and fresh produce. Genuine village feel, no tourist trap.

Google Maps

Mercadillo de Jávea

Thursdays · Morning · Jávea harbour

Clothes, accessories and local produce by the port. Easy to combine with a morning at the Arenal or old town.

Google Maps

Gata de Gorgos

Shops open all week · 15 min from Dénia

Unique in Spain. An entire village of wicker and rattan workshops selling directly from the maker. A completely different kind of visit.

Google Maps

Section five

Things not tomiss.

Dénia

Castillo de Dénia

Cultural · 3 € · 10h–18h

Dénia's landmark castle with panoramic views over the port and sea. Inside: an archaeological museum and a Civil War air-raid tunnel carved into the rock — not to be missed.

Google Maps

Cova Tallada by kayak

Activity · 35–50 €/person

A spectacular sea cave paddled to from Les Rotes — about 45 minutes from shore. Snorkel included. The single best activity in the area.

Google Maps

E-bike route · Montgó Natural Park

Activity · 40 km · ~55–65 €/person · Guide & bike included

40 km guided ride through the Montgó park between Dénia and Jávea. Stunning scenery and sea views throughout.

Book on GetYourGuide
Jávea

Cap de Sant Antoni · Jávea Lighthouse

Walking · Free · Open 24h · Best at sunset

Easy walk to the lighthouse at the tip of Jávea. Some of the best coastal views in the region.

Google Maps

San Bartolomé Church & Gothic old town

Cultural · Free

Gothic-Levantine architecture unlike anywhere else on the Costa Blanca. Authentic, quiet and never overcrowded.

Google Maps
Altea

Old Town · The Blue Domes

Cultural · Free · The finest old town on the Costa Blanca

Whitewashed houses, cobbled streets and blue domes. Known as "the dome of the Mediterranean." Visit before 11h or after 19h.

Google Maps

Portal Vell · 17th-century gateway

Monument · Listed cultural heritage · Free

The entrance to Altea's walled enclosure, built in 1617. Starting point for the most beautiful walk in the village.

Google Maps

Mirador de Altea

Views · Free · Do not miss at sunset

The best panoramic viewpoint in Altea. Extraordinary sea views at golden hour.

Google Maps

Art galleries

Cultural · Free · Throughout the old town

Altea has a long artistic tradition with galleries tucked into every corner of the old town. Worth wandering without a plan.
